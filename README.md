ProjExpert- 'Research Project Management System' is a joined effort in collaboration with Asst. Prof. Tribikram Pradhan of Tezpur University and the details of which can be drawn from the document titled ResearchProjectManagementSystem.pdf and the powerpoint presentation titled ProjExpert.pdf


It focuses on meeting the following requirements
ProjExpert can register Users, Staff, and Admins in
the system, making it easier to manage and track research projects, enhance communication
between stakeholders, and maintain project documentation.
1. User/Staff/Admin registration, User/Staff/Admin login credential authentication.
2. Users/Staff/Admin need to fill required attributes to register.
3. Admin can manage panel members, assign panel members based on specialization,
and send notices.
4. Users (Researchers) can form research groups, submit project proposals, and select
supervisors.
5. Supervisors can view and manage project proposals, assign co-supervisors, and
provide feedback.
6. Co-Supervisors can collaborate with supervisors and research groups, providing
additional guidance and feedback.
7. Panel Members can evaluate research topics and projects, providing assessments and
recommendations.
8. Users can chat in real-time with their supervisors and co-supervisors, and receive
notifications for project updates.
9. Users can submit project-related documents, track submission status, and monitor
project progress.
10. Admin can manage and view all project topics and statuses, send notices to specific
users or staff, and maintain overall system oversight.
